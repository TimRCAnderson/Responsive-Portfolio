About Me

My name is Timothy Richard Clark Anderson. I have an Associates of Applied Sciences in Simulation and Game Development, and five years of experience working in a development environment. My development experience has included both Agile and Waterfall development workflows, and has often challenged me to learn new languages in order to accomplish my responsibilities.

My free time has encompassed a number of different activities. When I was younger, I learned to sail in an optimist (a type of very small sailboat). I’ve always had a knack for technology, and I’m currently a member of Hackerspace Charlotte. I’ve also learned basic blacksmithing, and I’m learning to draft in CAD programs for 3D printing. From time to time, I try my hand at cosplay, and that is constantly teaching me new things.
